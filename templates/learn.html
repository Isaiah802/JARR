What We Offer

We sell both used and one-trip shipping containers in and around La Vernia, TX. Our containers are perfect for storage, moving, and a variety of other purposes. We offer a wide selection of sizes, from 20ft to 40ft containers. If you can't find the right container in our inventory, we can special order one for you upon request.

Shipping Information

We offer free shipping for containers delivered within a 50-mile radius of La Vernia. For locations further than 50 miles, additional shipping charges will apply. We strive to provide affordable, timely delivery to ensure your needs are met.

Why Choose JARR Containers?

As a relatively new startup, we are dedicated to providing great customer service and competitive prices. Our team has the expertise and commitment to meet your container needs. Whether you're looking to buy a container or need one delivered, we are here to help!
